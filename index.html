About KAYAKALP CLINIC

Bringing Ancient Wisdom to Modern Wellness

At KAYAKALP CLINIC, we believe in the power of holistic healing approaches that have been refined over thousands of years. Our name "Kayakalp" refers to the rejuvenation of body, mind, and spirit - the foundation of true wellness.

Founded in 2010 by Dr. Arya Sharma, our clinic combines time-tested Eastern therapeutic practices with modern wellness approaches to create personalized treatment plans for each client.

Our team of certified practitioners brings decades of experience in traditional Chinese medicine, Korean healing arts, and Ayurvedic practices, ensuring you receive the highest quality care for your specific needs.

Chinese Moxibustion

About Chinese Moxibustion

Moxibustion is a traditional Chinese medicine technique that involves burning dried mugwort (moxa) on particular points on the body. The purpose is to stimulate the flow of Qi (vital energy), strengthen the blood, and maintain general health.

Benefits:
  • Relieves chronic pain
  • Improves circulation
  • Strengthens immune system
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Supports digestive health
Treatment Details:

Duration: 45-60 minutes

Recommended: Initial series of 4-6 sessions

Price: ₹1,800 per session

Herbal Steam Therapy

About Herbal Steam Therapy

Herbal steam therapy uses carefully selected medicinal herbs and plants that release therapeutic compounds when heated. As you inhale the steam, these compounds help open airways, clear congestion, and promote detoxification through the skin.

Benefits:
  • Opens respiratory passages
  • Detoxifies through the skin
  • Relieves muscle tension
  • Improves circulation
  • Enhances relaxation and stress relief
Treatment Details:

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Recommended: Weekly sessions for optimal results

Korean Acupuncture

About Korean Acupuncture

Korean acupuncture is distinguished by its unique approach to diagnosis and treatment. It emphasizes the Saam Four Needle Technique, which uses fewer needles than traditional Chinese acupuncture and focuses on specific energetic relationships between the meridians.

Benefits:
  • Pain relief and management
  • Stress reduction
  • Improved sleep quality
  • Enhanced immune function
  • Hormonal balance
Treatment Details:

Duration: 60 minutes

Recommended: Initial series of 6-8 treatments

Price: ₹2,200 per session

Cupping Therapy

About Cupping Therapy

Cupping therapy is an ancient form of alternative medicine where therapists place special cups on the skin to create suction. This suction facilitates healing by improving blood flow, reducing inflammation, and promoting relaxation.

Benefits:
  • Relieves muscle tension and pain
  • Improves blood circulation
  • Aids in detoxification
  • Reduces inflammation
  • Promotes tissue healing
Treatment Details:

Duration: 30-45 minutes

Recommended: 1-2 sessions per month

Price: ₹1,600 per session
